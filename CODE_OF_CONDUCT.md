# Code of Conduct

## 1. Pledge

We, as contributors and maintainers of the **Aira** project, are committed to fostering an open, inclusive, and respectful community.

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

---

## 2. Standards of Conduct

### Acceptable Behavior

Contributors are expected to:

* Use respectful and inclusive language
* Be considerate of differing viewpoints and experiences
* Provide and accept constructive feedback professionally
* Demonstrate empathy and collaboration
* Act in the best interest of the community and project

### Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

* Harassment, discrimination, or offensive comments
* Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory remarks
* Trolling or disruptive behavior
* Public or private harassment
* Sharing private information without explicit permission
* Any conduct deemed inappropriate in a professional environment

---

## 3. Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:

* GitHub repositories
* Issues and pull requests
* Discussions and community channels
* Any official communication related to the project

It also applies when an individual is representing the project in public spaces.

---

## 4. Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.

Maintainers have the authority to:

* Remove or modify content that violates these guidelines
* Reject contributions that do not align with community standards
* Temporarily or permanently restrict participation of individuals who violate the Code of Conduct

All enforcement actions will be carried out in a fair and consistent manner.

---

## 5. Reporting

If you experience or witness any violation of this Code of Conduct, please report it to the project maintainers.

Contact: **[[bavanethamr@gmail.com](mailto:bavanethamr@gmail.com)]**

All reports will be handled confidentially and reviewed promptly.

---

## 6. Enforcement Guidelines

When addressing violations, maintainers may take the following actions based on severity:

1. **Warning** – Informing the individual of the violation
2. **Temporary Restriction** – Limiting participation for a defined period
3. **Permanent Ban** – Removal from the project community
